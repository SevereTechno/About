About Severe Techno

Severe Techno is a brand that has evolved over the years, starting from its early days of research and development to becoming a recognized name in technology. Here’s our journey:

  • 2004: Research began, leading to the establishment of Severe Technologies.
  • March 2010: The first website was launched for Microsoft Office downloads.
  • 2009: A side channel was created for various websites, starting with Bhatt Tech.
  • 2017: Another branch, Niragle, was added to expand the scope of services, leading Bhatt Tech to rename to Niragle.
  • 2020: Niragle was relaunched with a new name, Niral Studios and website.
  • 2016: The official YouTube channel for Severe Techno, as Severe Technologies was launched, marking the brand's entry officially.

Key Changes in Branding

  • The name Severe Techno unintentionally started to replace Severe Technologies in 2010 due to text length restrictions in URL for my first website, a website for Downloading the latest version of Microsoft Office.
  • The journey progressed further with the rebranding of the YouTube channel to NiraWare in 2022.
  • The deleted channel was replaced with a new channel with name Geeky Studios.
  • The channel was further renamed to Geeky People and later to Geeky Neeru.
  • The channel was later renamed Severe Techno in July 2024 to align with the brand identity.

YouTube Channel History

Severe Techno's YouTube journey has seen ups and downs:

  • Started as Severe Technologies on September 21, 2016.
  • Rebranded to NiraWare in December 2022.
  • On May 11, 2024, the channel was removed by YouTube due to a false allegation of “dangerous content”, which was incorrect as the content did not violate any guidelines.
  • A new channel named Geeky Studios was launched on May 21, 2024.
  • By July 17, 2024, the brand returned to its roots with the name Severe Techno.

Looking Ahead

Severe Techno continues to innovate, adapt, and grow. From hosting useful tools to exploring new horizons, we’re dedicated to providing value to our users and community.
